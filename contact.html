Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can you help with my computer issue?

For urgent issues, we strive to provide same-day remote support when contacted during business hours. For on-site visits, we typically schedule appointments within 24-48 hours depending on our current workload and your location.

What information should I have ready when contacting support?

It's helpful to provide: your device type and operating system, a detailed description of the issue including any error messages, when the problem started, and any troubleshooting steps you've already tried.

Do you offer service packages or just one-time help?

We offer both one-time service options and monthly maintenance packages for ongoing support. Our maintenance plans are ideal for small businesses or individuals who want regular system checks and priority support.

Is there a minimum charge for services?

Yes, we have a one-hour minimum for both remote and on-site support. After the first hour, we bill in 30-minute increments. We'll always provide a clear estimate before beginning any work.

Our Response Time Commitment

At 3thSec, we understand that technology issues can be frustrating and time-sensitive. Here's what you can expect when you reach out to us:

  • Email inquiries: Response within 24 business hours
  • Phone calls during business hours: Immediate response or callback within 2 hours
  • Urgent issues: Priority handling for critical problems affecting business operations
  • After-hours messages: Response by noon the next business day

We prioritize clear communication throughout the process and will keep you updated on the status of your support request.
